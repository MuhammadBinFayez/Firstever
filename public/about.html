About Me

I am Malik al-Faris, a warrior of the faith, sworn to uphold justice and defend the oppressed. Born above the golden sands of Al-Andalus, I was raised in the halls of wisdom, where scholars and warriors alike shaped my destiny. With my scimitar in hand and the guidance of the Almighty in my heart, I have ridden through the deserts of Arabia, the mountains of Persia, and the fertile lands of the Levant, fighting against tyranny and upholding the honor of my people.

My name is whispered in the courts of sultans and feared in the strongholds of invaders. I do not seek conquest for greed, nor war for glory—only the preservation of truth, knowledge, and the way of righteousness. Wherever darkness seeks to shroud the world, I shall ride forth like a beacon of light, unyielding, unwavering, and ever faithful to my cause.
